# 512-Compliant Consultant

A **512-Compliant Consultant** helps organizations adapt their systems, workflows, and contracts to operate under explicit execution-time constraints.

This is not regulatory compliance.
It is **execution fitness**.

---

## What This Role Does

512-Compliant Consultants:

- identify workflows exposed to machine-speed execution
- locate hidden dependence on discretion and exception
- refactor processes to pass binary admissibility checks
- redesign systems to fail fast instead of dispute later

They work with reality as defined — not negotiated.

---

## What This Role Is Not

This role does not:
- grant approvals
- certify compliance
- override constraints
- interpret failures

If a workflow cannot pass 512, it must change or move downstream.

---

## Typical Engagements

- execution boundary audits
- constraint refactoring workshops
- exception elimination programs
- partner and vendor readiness preparation

Deliverables are concrete and binary.

---

## Ideal Backgrounds

- compliance and risk professionals
- auditors
- legal operations
- enterprise architects

Experience managing edge cases is the asset — now applied upstream.

---

## Final Note

512-Compliant Consultants do not make systems safer by adding controls.

They make systems safe by **removing ambiguity**.
