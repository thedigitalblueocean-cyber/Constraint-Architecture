# CVS Specialist

A **CVS Specialist** works with execution evidence after it exists.

This role exists because CVS is deliberately disjoint from execution.

---

## What This Role Does

CVS Specialists:

- analyze execution records
- perform forensic reconstruction
- support audit and attribution
- enable deterministic replay

They do not intervene in execution.

---

## What This Role Is Not

CVS Specialists do not:
- block actions
- enforce policy
- control systems
- provide runtime governance

They work exclusively on evidence.

---

## Ideal Backgrounds

- audit professionals
- forensic analysts
- compliance investigators
- systems analysts

---

## Final Note

CVS Specialists replace narrative explanations with evidence.

They do not decide what should have happened.

They show what did.
