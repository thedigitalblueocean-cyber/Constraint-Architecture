# CVS — Cryptographic Verification System

CVS is a disjoint evidence plane that records execution without influencing it.

---

## Properties

CVS is:
- passive
- immutable
- replayable
- attribution-preserving

---

## What CVS Is Not

CVS is not:
- governance
- enforcement
- monitoring
- prevention

It observes only.

---

## Purpose

To replace narrative reconstruction with mechanical evidence.

---

## Final Note

CVS does not teach systems how to behave.

It shows them how they behaved.
